AIS,

There seems to be a bit of confusion about Zayne Malik leaving the popular spunky boy band, One-Direction. Many are now calling it .8 Direction, but they are gravely mistaken.

One direction denotes the change in position of a vector, not the magnitude. So technically they're still one direction, just that the modulus of the new 1D will be 4/5 (or .8) the modulus of the old 1D.

Use this tid-bit of information to make small talk at your internship this Summer. You're sure to impress.
